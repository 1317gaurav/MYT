    About Us:

    At Taboola, our brand is our story, the reason we go to work every day, the things that we stand for.
    We believe that by adhering to our story in all the work that we do, we can build a truly great company.
    A company that innovates, leads, and helps our employees and clients succeed.

    4 Company Strategic Goals for the next 4 Years (2020-2023)

    • Make advertisers successful on Taboola
    • Defend & grow our position as leaders on the open web
    • Measurment Helps Focus Work
    • Earn 10X user attention
    • Empower Taboolars to be better together

    Mission Statement:

    People have been and always will be the key to our success. Taboola’s unique culture is our secret sauce: we are bright, kind, curious, and above all, wildly passionate. We believe that the best ideas are backed by data and are brought to life by drive, ownership, and teamwork.
    We believe the secret to Taboola’s success is in its innovative spirit and our employees’ courage to innovate, create, and try new things without the fear of failure.

    Our Corporate Values:

    • Partners First
             Our first priority is the success of our partners, both advertisers and digital properties. If our partners succeed, we succeed.
    • An Entrepreneurial Culture
             We believe the secret to Taboola’s success is in its innovative spirit. We take the initiative, get things done, and push to accomplish what’s next.
    • Data-Driven Results
             We pride ourselves in using data to make our products and decisions better. Measurement helps focus work so we continue to succeed.
    • Support for The Open Internet
             We value the diversity and importance of the open web. Everything we do is in support of a thriving internet ecosystem.
    • Unity Through Diversity
             Taboola is a global company, and we thrive on learning from others. We value the power of diverse viewpoints combining into one focused team.
