    Travel

    The purpose of this policy is to ensure consistent and fair treatment of employees required to travel on behalf of Taboola. The guiding principles are:

    • Business Travels will be booked by Employees based on the guidelines set forth in this policy and through the travel tool.
    • All business travels falling outside of the approved guidelines will be subject to the approval of the regional travel coordinators, with the decision facilitated by Eldad Maniv, COO or David Aber, CFO.

    Employee Referral Program

    Who is considered an “eligible employee” that can refer a candidate? All Taboola employees (including interns and students) are eligible to participate in our referral program except for:

    • C-level management
    • VPs and above
    • HR department team members

    POSH - Prevention Of Sexual Harassment

    Taboola is committed to providing a work environment that is free of illicit harassment. As a result, the Company maintains a strict policy prohibiting sexual harassment and harassment, against applicants and employees based on any legally-recognized basis, including, but not limited to: race, color, religion, sex, creed,

    Health Insurance

    The Policy will pay for medical expenses as are reasonably and necessarily incurred on account of such treatment, subject to certain terms and conditions and including but not restricted to:

    • Room Charges
    • Nursing expenses
    • Surgeon, Anesthetist, Medical Practitioner, Consultant, Specialists Fees
    • Anesthesia , Blood, Oxygen , Operation Theatre Charges, Surgical Appliances, Medicines & Drugs, Diagnostic Materials & X-Ray, Dialysis, Chemotherapy, Radiotherapy, Cost of Pacemaker & similar expenses
